Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann

Gurren Lagann, known in Japan as Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann (Japanese: 天元突破グレンラガン, Hepburn: Tengen Toppa Guren Ragan, lit. "Heaven-Piercing Gurren Lagann"), is a Japanese mecha anime television series animated by Gainax and co-produced by Aniplex and Konami.

Resume

The story is set in a distant future where humanity is forced to live underground due to the surface being ruled by the oppressive Spiral King Lordgenome and his army of giant mechs known as Gunmen. The protagonist, Simon, and his friend Kamina discover a mysterious Gunman buried deep underground and use it to escape to the surface. They embark on a journey to defy the oppressive rule and explore the world above
